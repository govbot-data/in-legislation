SENATE BILL No. 183
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 6-3-2-29.5.
Synopsis: Income tax deduction for health insurance premiums.
Provides that a taxpayer who is covered by a health insurance plan may
deduct from the taxpayer's adjusted gross income an amount equal to
the total amount of premiums on the health insurance plan the taxpayer
paid for coverage during the taxable year.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
