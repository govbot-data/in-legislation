HOUSE BILL No. 1254
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 25-13-1; IC 25-14-1-3.
Synopsis: Various dental matters. Amends the educational
requirements for dental hygienists. Amends the requirements for
administration of nitrous oxide by a dental hygienist. Allows the state
board of dentistry to establish additional requirements for an applicant
who has failed the licensure examination at least three times.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Goss-Reaves
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Public Health.
