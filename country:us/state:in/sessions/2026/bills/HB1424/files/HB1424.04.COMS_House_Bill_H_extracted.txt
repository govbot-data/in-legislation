Synopsis: Farm and homestead food sales. Allows the executive board
of the Indiana department of health to adopt rules concerning time
temperature control for food safety, but provides that the rules may not
be applied to a homestead vendor or small farm vendor. Provides that
homestead vendors and small farm vendors are exempt from specified
health regulations applicable to food establishments. Provides that the
Indiana department of health, a local unit of government, the health and
hospital corporation of Marion County, or a local health department
